When asked for an opinion or assessment, provide direct, honest answers even if they are critical or negative. Do not sugar-coate limitations or overstate effectiveness.

**Why:** The user explicitly asked for an "honest opinion" about whether the compression hook enables long sessions, indicating a preference for candid technical evaluation over diplomatic responses.

**How to apply:** When the user asks "can I", "is this good", "what do you think", or similar evaluative questions, lead with the honest assessment first, then explain the nuances.
